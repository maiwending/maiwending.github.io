abstract: 'Power integrity (PI) problem is essential when analyzing high speed signal passing through power ground. The fundamental mode in power ground is the zero-order parallel plate mode, which is capable for 2D simplification. However, in areas around anti-pads and other z-axis discontinuities, 3D algorithm has to be adopted to improve the accuracy. A hybrid 2D／3D discontinuous Galerkin time domain (DGTD) method has advantage on both accuracy and efficiency, thus is effective to cope with such full wave simulations. The 2D and 3D domains share the same triangular prism mesh. With appropriated basis functions, different domains can couple with each other…'
